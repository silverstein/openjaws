### Fish Market
- **Awning Stripes**: Red (#FF6B6B) and White (#FFFFFF)

- **Sign Text**: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Price Text**: Gold (#FFD700)
- **Prompt**: Green (#00FF00)
- **Selection Glow**: Gold (#FFD700)

- **Sardine**: Silver (#C0C0C0)

- **Tuna**: Tomato Red (#FF6347)

- **Ripples**: Teal (#4ECDC4)
- **Fish Chunks**: Orange (#FFA500)
